Who is DenverSEO.io?
DenverSEO.io is the dedicated AI search and SEO practice operated by Eye To Ad Media, a Denver-based conversion-focused tech marketing agency founded in 2012 by Zach Wennstedt. Specifically, we engineer business visibility across nine AI search engines plus classical Google, ship measurable conversion improvements, and report tied to revenue rather than rankings. Above all, we put customer success first.

Eye To Ad Media has operated in the Denver market since 2012. Specifically, we built the agency around three principles that the broader SEO industry routinely violates: tie reporting to revenue, engineer for conversion not just traffic, and keep the senior strategist who sold the work involved throughout. Furthermore, the discipline that worked for classical SEO continues to work for AI search optimization — the surfaces changed, the principles did not.

DenverSEO.io exists because the modern search landscape requires a focused practice. Specifically, optimizing across Google, AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Manus, and emerging agentic platforms is a different discipline than the SEO most agencies still sell. As a result, we built a dedicated brand for the work and dedicated playbooks for each engine. Our network of pages documents the methodology publicly because we believe transparency is the prerequisite for trust.

How We Work

Three Operating Principles, No Exceptions.

1

Customer Success First

Specifically, every engagement metric, deliverable, and conversation centers on whether the work is moving the customer's business. Furthermore, ranking improvements that do not tie to revenue do not earn space in our reports.

2

Conversion Over Traffic

Traffic without conversion is expensive theater. Specifically, every campaign integrates conversion engineering — form optimization, pricing transparency, agent-readable infrastructure — alongside visibility work. As a result, the same investment produces compounding returns.
