What Is Generative Engine Optimization in Denver?

Quick Answer
What is generative engine optimization Denver?
Generative engine optimization Denver, often shortened to GEO, is the discipline of engineering a Denver business into AI-generated answers. AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews now answer most buyer questions directly. GEO ensures your business is the answer, which produces real leads and new customers.

GEO is a real discipline. The term was coined by academic researchers in late 2023. The original GEO research paper proposed specific tactics that produced measurable lifts in AI citation rates. Since then, GEO has become the formal name for the work of optimizing for AI search.

For Denver businesses, GEO matters for one reason. The buyer journey now starts with AI. Customers ask ChatGPT for recommendations. They use Perplexity to research vendors. They follow Google AI Overview suggestions. The traditional path of typing a query and clicking the top result has shifted. AI is the new front door.

The Origin of GEO

The term "generative engine optimization" was coined in late 2023 by academic researchers studying how to optimize content for large language model citation. The original GEO research paper proposed nine specific tactics. Several of those tactics produced citation lifts of 30 to 40 percent. This was not theory. It was measured impact.

Since then, GEO has become the formal industry name. Practitioners often call the same work "AEO" or "AI search optimization." The vocabulary varies by audience. The actual engineering does not. We use whichever term the buyer prefers because the work produces the same result either way.

GEO vs AEO — Are They the Same?

GEO and answer engine optimization Denver, or AEO, are closely related. The terms are often used interchangeably. The small distinction is this. GEO emphasizes the formal academic discipline. AEO emphasizes the practical work of getting cited in answer boxes. We do both. The vocabulary varies. The work is the same.

GEO matters because it changes how leads are generated. Old SEO produced traffic. Some of that traffic became leads. GEO produces leads directly. When a buyer asks an AI engine for a recommendation, the AI names a business. The buyer calls. The business gets a lead. Skip GEO and your competitors get those leads instead.
