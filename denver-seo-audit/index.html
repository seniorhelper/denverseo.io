Local SEO Audit

Your Denver Map Pack Audit — Are You Winning Local?

The Google Map Pack — the three business listings that appear in a box above organic results for local searches — captures the majority of clicks on high-intent local queries. "HVAC repair Denver," "dentist near me," "roofing company Denver" — whoever owns those three spots owns the market for those searches.

Your local SEO audit covers every factor that determines map pack placement. Google Business Profile completeness — categories, service areas, business attributes, photo freshness, and Q&A content. Review profile health — volume, recency, response rate, and sentiment patterns. Citation consistency — whether your NAP data is correct and consistent across the directories Google uses to verify business legitimacy.

We also benchmark your local presence against the top three map pack results in your specific Denver market. Where are they stronger? What would it take to displace them? The audit answers these questions with data — not guesses.

Furthermore, as AI search and local SEO increasingly intersect, your Google Business Profile data is now being used by AI systems to generate local business recommendations. A strong local SEO foundation therefore strengthens both your map pack position and your AI search visibility simultaneously.
